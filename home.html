To Guide a Children

A home for Orphaned girl children where they become our children.Kanavagam is supported by Tamil Nadu Foundation.With Kanavagam, we are striving for an orphanage that offers the absolute best for the children. The organization is built on three foundational pillars; health, education and character-building. We currently have 12 girl children and by this May we will reach a capacity of 25 children.
